Tips and suggestions

To highlight a word with bold, type ‹b› before the word and ‹/b› after the word. To italicise a word, write ‹i› before the word and ‹/i› after the word. To start a new paragraph, write ‹p› at the beginning of the paragraph and ‹/p› at the end.

To change forum

Each conference has at least one forum, with ongoing discussions. Choose a forum and click OK.

To read discussions

This is the header 03-20 16:59 [4]

The header shown in the left part of the window under CURRENT CONTRIBUTION is the initial contribution submitted to each respective discussion.

The symbol shown in front of the header informs that there is either a new discussion or that new contributions have been submitted since you last visited the conference.

After the "New" symbol, the discussion header is displayed with the publication date and time as well as the number of contributions to the discussion.

By clicking the discussion header, all contributions will be shown in the right part of the window.

To read contributions

Header: [ The header of the contribution ]
In my opinion ...
Written by John Brown 2000-03-21 15:47 (yyyy-mm-dd, time)

All contributions to the discussion are presented to the right of the window. The symbol X shown in front of the header implies that the contribution is written by a user who takes part in this conference in an expert capacity.

After an "expert symbol" follows the header, text, author, and date of publication.

Sort: Choice of sorting order of contributions. "Reverse chronological order" shows the latest contribution first, and "Chronological order" shows the latest contributions last.

To create discussions / comments

To create a discussion, click "New discussion"; write a title or headline in the HEADER field and the body in the TEXT field. To close, click "Submit" button. Use "Reset" button to terminate without saving your contribution.

To make a contribution to a discussion, click "Contribute"; write your message in the TEXT field and close by clicking the "Submit" button. Use the "Reset" button to terminate without saving your contribution.

Other: To include a link or mail click these. A dialogue box will open where the web address or mail address for the text field is written. When "OK" button is pressed, the HTML code needed to insert the link is automatically created.

To browse

Discussions shown in discussion box are the latest additions. To search for earlier discussions, use buttons "Earlier" and "Later" which enables you to browse through all discussions.

To update contributions

To show contributions added since your latest login, use "Update" button

To search

Search maybe made among text headers, contribution text and users of a specific forum. Type in the search word and press button "Search". The search may be limited by stating start and end dates. A date is written as year-month-day (yyyy-mm-dd), or as "yesterday" and "today". Example of search date: 2000-04-02.
