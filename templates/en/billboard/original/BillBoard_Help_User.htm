Changing sections

Every billboard has at least one section, there various notices can be posted. Select which section you wish to visit and then click on "Select".


Reading notices

This is the header 03-20 16:59 [4]

The notices displayed in the window's left half are the headers of the most recently posted notices in each section.

When you click on the a notice's header on the left half of your browser window, the notice with its full text appears in the right half of the window.


Create a notice/ reply
A notice is created by clicking on "NEW NOTICE", writing in your message in the form that appears and finishing by pushing "Save". Use the "Undo" button to cancel without saving the notice.
A reply is created when you click on "Reply", writing in your message in the form that appears and finishing by pushing the "Send" button. Use the "Undo" button to cancel the reply and stop it from being sent.

Other information about creating notices: Between the header field and text field there are two links, "LINKS" and "E-MAIL". When you push on one of these, a dialogue box will appear so that you can add a web address/e-mail address which will be included in the notice's text. When you click on"OK" HTML code is automatically created for these links and pasted into the text for you.

Looking through the sections
 The sections shown in the list of sections are those most recently created. To see older sections use the "Later" och "Earlier" buttons which allow you to look through the other sections.


Updating notices
To display the notices that have been posted since you logged in push the "Update" button.

Searching the billboard
Searches can be made of texts, headers, content and authors within a particular section. Enter search word and push the "Search" button. The search can be limited by giving different start and end dates for the search period. Dates are given in the form of year-month-day (YYYY-MM-DD), or in the special form "yesterday" and "today".
For example at search date could be: 2002-11-19
